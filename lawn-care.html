Hiring Lawn Care Guide

Why It Matters

Professional lawn care enhances curb appeal, protects property value, and promotes environmental stewardship through proper maintenance practices.

Types of Services

  • Basic mowing and maintenance
  • Fertilization and weed control programs
  • Aeration and overseeding
  • Irrigation system maintenance
  • Tree and shrub care

Licensing & Insurance

  • Pesticide applicator licensing (for chemical treatments)
  • Liability insurance
  • Business licensing

Evaluating Providers

  • Customized plans vs. one-size-fits-all
  • Soil testing offered
  • Knowledge of local grass types and climate

Understanding Programs

  • What each application includes
  • Timing and frequency
  • Granular vs. liquid treatments
  • Organic and pet-safe options

Pricing Structures

  • Per-visit vs. annual contracts
  • Square footage calculations
  • Add-on services and upsells

Questions to Ask

  • How do you handle problem areas?
  • What happens if treatments don't work?
  • How do you communicate service visits?
  • What's your cancellation policy?

Environmental Considerations

  • Pollinator-safe practices
  • Water conservation approaches
  • Organic alternatives

Tip: Ask for a soil test before starting any fertilization program. This ensures treatments address your lawn's specific needs.
