Hiring a Plumber Guide

Why It Matters

Choosing the right plumber is critical. Poor plumbing work can lead to water damage costing thousands in repairs, code violations, and long-term maintenance issues.

Licensing & Credentials

  • Verify state and local licensing requirements
  • Confirm liability insurance coverage
  • Check workers' compensation insurance
  • Ask about bonding status

Experience & Specialization

  • Residential vs. commercial focus
  • Specific expertise: water heaters, repiping, sewer lines, fixtures

Getting Estimates

  • Understand flat-rate vs. hourly pricing
  • Ask about diagnostic fees
  • Request written estimates with detailed breakdowns

Red Flags to Avoid

  • Pressure to decide immediately
  • Cash-only payment requests
  • No written contract provided
  • Unwillingness to pull permits

Questions to Ask

  • What warranty do you offer on parts and labor?
  • What is the timeline for completion?
  • Who will perform the work - owner, employees, or subcontractors?

Emergency Services

  • 24/7 availability and after-hours rates
  • Response time expectations

Tip: Always get at least 3 quotes and verify each contractor's license through Alabama's licensing board before hiring.
