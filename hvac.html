Hiring an HVAC Contractor Guide

Why It Matters

Your HVAC system affects comfort, energy efficiency, and indoor air quality. Proper installation and maintenance are essential for performance and longevity.

Licensing & Certifications

  • EPA 608 certification (refrigerant handling)
  • NATE certification (industry standard)
  • State contractor licensing

Assessing Your Needs

  • Repair vs. replacement decision factors
  • System sizing (Manual J load calculation)
  • Efficiency ratings: SEER, AFUE, HSPF

Getting Quotes

  • Insist on in-home assessments (avoid phone-only quotes)
  • Ask about equipment brands offered
  • Installation quality matters as much as equipment

Maintenance Plans

  • What's included: annual tune-ups, priority service, discounts
  • Review contract terms and cancellation policies

Energy Efficiency

  • Ask about rebates and tax credits
  • Ductwork inspection and sealing
  • Smart thermostat compatibility

Red Flags

  • Dramatically lower bids (may indicate shortcuts)
  • Oversized or undersized unit recommendations
  • High-pressure upselling tactics
  • No load calculation performed

Tip: A properly sized system is crucial. Oversized units cycle too frequently, while undersized ones run constantly. Insist on a Manual J calculation.
